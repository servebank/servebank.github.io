  • Step 1: Submit Company Information

    I want to submit my Company Information

    All info will be kept strictly confidential

    We will review your company info
    Our review team will contact you for a meeting
    We do not meet you unless we have received your company info.

  • Step 2: Review Process

    We will discuss with you the listing process.
    We will discuss with you the agreement terms.

  • Step 3: Pre-listing Process

    We will work with you to prepare the followings:

    1. Business Plan
    2. Audited Financials
    3. Business Documents
    4. Writing, Editing & Drafting of Prospectus
    5. Respond to SEC comments

    6. Acquire Market Marker / Ticker Symbols
    7. Subscription Agreements
    8. Acquire Stock Transfer Agents
    9. All other compliance / requirements from the SEC

  • Step 4: Post-listing Process

    What YOU do:

    Manage entire business
    Meet financial target

    What WE do:

    Annual SEC Compliance
    Public Relations
    Investor Relations
    Share Registrations
